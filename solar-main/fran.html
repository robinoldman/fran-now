Between 2009 and 2016, he reported from across South and Southeast Asia on democratization and military coups, conflict and large-scale displacement, for US and European magazines and newspapers.

In addition, he has written on the impact of violent rhetoric on UK politics, the psychology of climate protest movements, the colonial origins of postcolonial violence, the effect of the climate crisis on citizenship and legal status, and the failures of the UN in contexts of ethnic cleansing and genocide.
